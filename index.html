About Us

DisposeALL is a locally owned and operated junk removal company in North Vancouver. Owned by Kai Nielsen and Ty Kurucz, the business launched in 2013 with the aim to provide a professional and economical disposal service to local residents. We have over four years of experience in the industry as we are a sister company to Brother Nature Landscaping, founded in 2010. Noticing the need for honest and reliable service in the area, we set out to create a sustainable company that will contribute to the local economy. Our ultimate goal is to leave our customers with piece of mind and provide a genuine service not easily matched.
